About Us

Our Story

Born out of a passion for great food, exceptional hospitality, and the desire to create an oasis of taste and elegance in the bustling city, Slice'd Pizzeria was founded with a simple yet ambitious goal: to redefine the way you experience pizza. From the very beginning, we've set out to elevate this beloved Italian classic to new heights.

Our Commitment to You

What sets Slice'd Pizzeria apart is our commitment to craftsmanship and quality. Our culinary team, guided by years of experience and a deep appreciation for fresh, locally-sourced ingredients, crafts each pizza with precision and love. Our hand-tossed dough, signature sauces, and carefully selected toppings come together to create a symphony of flavors that will leave your taste buds dancing.

The Slice'd Experience

But it's not just about the food. At Slice'd, we believe that dining is an experience to be savored. That's why we've created a rooftop oasis that transports you to another world. Our rooftop dining area offers a breathtaking panoramic view of the city, making it the perfect setting for a romantic dinner, a celebration with friends, or a casual evening under the stars.

Join Us at Slice'd

Whether you're a pizza connoisseur looking for the finest slices in Hyderabad or seeking a unique dining experience that combines delectable cuisine with stunning views, Slice'd Pizzeria invites you to join us on this gastronomic journey. We're more than a pizzeria; we're your destination for exceptional dining in the heart of Banjara Hills.
