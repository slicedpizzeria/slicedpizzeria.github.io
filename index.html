About Us

Our Journey

Born out of a passion for great food and a desire to create a unique dining experience for every kind, Slice'd started out as a humble pizzeria with a simple yet ambitious goal: to redefine how you experience pizza. From the very beginning, we have set out to bring this Italian classic closer to everyone.

How we do it

The whole experience of Slice'd can be equated to the three tenets we follow. Globally Inspired: we have infused global flavors into a good ol' Italian product, pizza, and created something extraordinary. Healthier: We are committed to creating healthier Pizzas by using only 00'Morcote flour for our dough. Fresh: No frozen or processed food is used in our kitchen. We only use locally sourced fresh ingredients. Everything that goes onto our dishes is 100% in-house made and oven-baked.

The Slice'd Experience

It's not just about the food; at Slice'd, we believe that dining is an experience to be savored. So, we have created an exquisite rooftop dinner to give you an experience that will be cherished. Our rooftop dining area offers a breathtaking panoramic view of the city, making it a perfect setting for a romantic dinner, a celebration with friends, or a casual evening under the stars.
